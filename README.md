# Elsewhere & Co.

**Streetwear for people between places.**

We design for the in-between — the quiet tension of knowing where you’re from and building a life somewhere new. Elsewhere & Co. is modern, diaspora-bred apparel: heavy blanks, intentional graphics, and no costume drama. You carry more than one address in your head; your clothes can say that without shouting.

---

## Drop 01 — *Roots Remain*

Our opening collection is built around **city crossovers** — typography and storytelling for the routes you actually live. Tees and fleece celebrate the places you claim and the ones you leave crossed out: bold type, varsity nods, and graphics that feel rooted instead of borrowed.

Pieces are offered in small batches, with fabrics and fits chosen to feel honest on the body — everyday streetwear that respects distance, memory, and belonging.
